Free Mentor

MISSION

Free mentor is a platform to help young people who wish to turn their dream into reality. But that dream doesn’t come easily. It’s hard work, and there are challenges around every corner. this platform connects young people with the most experienced mentors across the globe. . It’s a free platform that connects young people with professional mentors so that they can solve problems and build the future together. young people can find a match on this platform that will offer them solutions.

VISION

Free Mentor is a platform designed to support young people to acquire 20th-century skills allowing your them to have a global impact. and give them knowledge to make them smart and take them to another level, it offers young people an easy way to have a growth mindset and achive their goals, you are required to sign up and search for your favorites mentor in a minute. Our platform is accessible from everywhere, young people can be mentored, no matter where they are located.

HOW IT WORKS

Sign up and complete your profile. Describe yourself, what you do, where you live , your age, your gender, your career, your experience, your fun fact,what you hope to accomplish here, Send messages to your mentor to express your interest in starting a new mentoring session. Talk on the phone or chat on whatsapp, facebook, twitter, linked in, git hub, skype, slack or instagram to get to know one another. Move forward by setting goals and solving problems together!
